About Agile AI University

Agile AI University operates as an independent Academic & Professional Body dedicated to defining capability frameworks, structured assessments, and professional standards for Agile AI and Agentic AI.

The institution exists to bring clarity and structure to a rapidly evolving domain where artificial intelligence increasingly influences decision-making, execution, governance, and leadership.

Institutional Positioning

Agile AI University is not positioned as a training provider, certification marketplace, or content platform.

Its primary role is to establish academically grounded frameworks, assessment references, and professional alignment models that support experienced professionals, organizations, and governance bodies.

Scope of Work

  • Defining Agile AI and Agentic AI capability frameworks
  • Publishing insight-based capability assessments
  • Establishing professional pathways for advanced practitioners
  • Governing credential standards and recognition models
