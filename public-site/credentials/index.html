What Our Credentials Represent

  • Validated professional capability aligned to institutional frameworks
  • Applied competence demonstrated beyond theoretical knowledge
  • Readiness for real-world execution, governance, or leadership roles

Credential Tracks

Professional Track (P Track)

Recognizes applied professional capability in Agile AI execution, AI-enabled decision systems, and organizational delivery contexts.

Master Track (M Track)

Recognizes advanced mastery in Agentic AI systems, orchestration, and governance at scale.

Leadership Track (L Track)

Recognizes strategic leadership capability in organizational transformation, AI governance, and enterprise-level decision stewardship.

What Credentials Are Not

  • They are not entry-level or foundational certifications
  • They are not issued for completing short assessments
  • They are not marketing incentives or participation rewards

Assessments and Credentials

Capability assessments offered by Agile AI University are diagnostic tools. They support self-reflection, pathway alignment, and professional insight.

Assessments do not confer credentials. Credentials are governed separately under formal academic and professional criteria.

Credential Verification

All credentials are issued digitally and may be verified through the official credential verification portal.

Verification portal: verify.agileai.university
