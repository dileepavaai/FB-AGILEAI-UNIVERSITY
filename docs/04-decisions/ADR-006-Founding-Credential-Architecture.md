# Summary

This Architectural Decision Record defines the lifecycle relationship between
academic programmes, professional credentials, recognitions and learner
progression within the Agile AI University ecosystem.

This decision establishes the Founding Credential Architecture and introduces
the concept of lifelong learner progression without altering historical
academic achievements.

This ADR is authoritative.

---

# Background

Agile AI University originally launched the

Agile Outcome Practitioner (AOP)

programme.

As the Agile AI ecosystem matured, the university introduced

Artificial Intelligence Professional Agilist (AIPA)

as the flagship professional credential.

This created an important architectural question.

Should an existing AOP credential be upgraded?

Or should the learner earn a completely new credential?

This ADR answers that question.

---

# Decision

The university SHALL preserve every earned credential permanently.

Programmes may evolve.

Programmes may retire.

Professional recognitions may evolve.

Academic credentials SHALL remain immutable.

Every significant learner achievement SHALL result in a new credential.

Existing credentials SHALL never be modified or overwritten.

---

# Founding Credential

The Agile Outcome Practitioner (AOP) credential is designated as the

Founding Credential of Agile AI University

of Agile AI University.

The programme itself is retired.

The credential remains permanently valid.

This designation recognises the original learners who established the early
professional community of Agile AI University.

---

# Programme Lifecycle

Programmes have an operational lifecycle.

Example

Active

↓

Retired

↓

Archived

Retiring a programme DOES NOT invalidate previously earned credentials.

Programme lifecycle and credential lifecycle are independent.

---

# Credential Lifecycle

Credentials have an academic lifecycle.

Draft

↓

Approved

↓

Issued

↓

Verified

↓

Permanent Academic Record

Credentials SHALL remain valid indefinitely unless formally revoked through
University governance procedures.

Credentials SHALL never be overwritten because a newer programme exists.

---

# Recognition Lifecycle

Professional recognition represents the learner's current professional standing.

Recognition MAY evolve.

Credentials SHALL NOT.

Example

Credential

Agile Outcome Practitioner

↓

Recognition

Artificial Intelligence Professional Agilist

The credential remains unchanged.

The learner's professional recognition evolves.

---

# Professional Progression

Learner progression is achieved by earning NEW credentials.

Example

Agile Outcome Practitioner (AOP)

↓

Bridge Programme

↓

Artificial Intelligence Professional Agilist (AIPA)

↓

Agentic AI Professional (AAIP)

↓

Agile AI Leadership (AIAL)

↓

Future Professional Credentials

Each milestone produces

• New Credential
• New Certificate
• New Badge
• New Verification Record

No existing credential is modified.

---

# Bridge Programmes

Bridge programmes create progression.

They do not replace history.

Completing a bridge programme results in

• A new credential
• A new certificate
• A new digital badge

The original credential remains permanently valid.

---

# Organic Growth Strategy

Every learner milestone becomes an opportunity for professional recognition.

Each progression generates

• Certificate
• Badge
• Verification
• LinkedIn Publication
• Professional Portfolio Update

This creates continuous organic visibility for Agile AI University.

The university therefore encourages lifelong progression rather than one-time certification.

---

# Founding Cohort

Learners who successfully completed the original AOP programme are recognised
as members of the

Founding Agile AI Cohort.

This designation is permanent.

Future learners cannot obtain this designation.

The Founding Cohort represents the earliest generation of Agile AI
University professionals.

---

# Governance Principles

The following principles are permanently adopted.

1.

Programmes may retire.

Credentials remain valid.

2.

Credentials are immutable academic records.

3.

Recognition may evolve independently.

4.

Every significant achievement produces a new credential.

5.

Historical credentials are never overwritten.

6.

Verification preserves academic history.

7.

Bridge programmes enable progression.

They do not replace credentials.

8.

The Student Portal presents professional journeys rather than individual certificates.

9.

The Credential Registry is the authoritative source of academic truth.

10.

Agile AI University promotes lifelong professional growth.

# Final Decision

LOCKED

Agile Outcome Practitioner (AOP)

is permanently recognised as the

Founding Credential of Agile AI University

of Agile AI University.
