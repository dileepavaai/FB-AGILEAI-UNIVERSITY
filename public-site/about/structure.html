Institutional Structure

Agile AI University operates through a structured separation of academic, assessment, and credential governance functions.

Functional Separation

  • Academic Function — frameworks, definitions, references
  • Assessment Function — insight-based capability instruments
  • Pathways Function — professional alignment guidance
  • Credential Governance — standards, eligibility, verification

Why This Structure Matters

Separating these functions ensures that no single activity improperly influences another.

This protects academic integrity, prevents credential dilution, and preserves long-term professional credibility.

Relationship to Programs

Learning programs and professional tracks may align to frameworks and pathways defined by Agile AI University.

However, program delivery remains distinct from academic governance and credential authority.
