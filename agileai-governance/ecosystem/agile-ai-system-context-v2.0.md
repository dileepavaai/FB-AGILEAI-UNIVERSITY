# 2. INSTITUTIONAL MODEL (LOCKED)

The Agile AI ecosystem operates on a **three-layer institutional model**:

Agile AI Foundation → Defines
Agile AI University → Evaluates & Credentials
Agile AI Academy → Enables Learning


---

## 2.1 Agile AI Foundation

Role:
- Domain definition authority  
- Publishes canonical frameworks  
- Defines governance principles  

Characteristics:
- Non-commercial  
- Static and stable  
- No training or certification  

---

## 2.2 Agile AI University

Role:
- Academic and professional body  
- Evaluates capability  
- Issues credentials  

Characteristics:
- Advisory and interpretive  
- Non-training institution  
- Credential authority  

---

## 2.3 Agile AI Academy

Role:
- Learning enablement layer  
- Cohort execution  
- Capability development support  

Characteristics:
- No credential authority  
- No evaluation authority  
- Execution-focused
