Institutional Overview

Agile AI University builds upon the canonical standards defined by the Agile AI Foundation, operationalizing structured capability architecture, governed assessment systems, and professional recognition models for AI-enabled agility.

The institutional mandate is architectural and governance-centered rather than commercial. Explicit structural separation is maintained between canonical standards (defined by the Foundation), capability architecture, assessment instrumentation, executive interpretation, and credential recognition.
