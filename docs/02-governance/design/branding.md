# Purpose

This document establishes the official branding standards for Agile AI University.

Branding ensures every interaction, interface, credential, publication and communication consistently represents the University's institutional identity.

Branding extends beyond logos and colors.

It governs perception.

---

# Scope

This specification applies to:

* Public Website
* Student & Executive Portal
* Administration Portal
* Credential Operations Suite
* Credential Registry
* Certificate Generator
* Badge Generator
* Credential Verification
* Recognition Services
* Certificates
* Digital Badges
* Documentation
* Marketing Materials
* Email Communications
* Presentation Templates
* Future Products

---

# Brand Philosophy

Agile AI University exists to advance professional capability in Agile AI and Agentic AI through rigorous academic governance, practical application and lifelong recognition.

The brand should consistently communicate:

* Trust
* Academic Excellence
* Professional Credibility
* Executive Maturity
* Modern Thinking
* Long-Term Value

---

# Institutional Positioning

Agile AI University is positioned as an independent Academic & Professional Body.

The institution is not positioned as:

* a commercial training company
* a coaching business
* a bootcamp
* a certification vendor
* a consulting firm

All branding shall reinforce this positioning.

---

# Brand Attributes

Every visual and written experience should reflect the following qualities:

* Academic
* Professional
* Executive
* Modern
* Calm
* Minimal
* Trustworthy
* Global
* Evidence-Based

---

# Brand Personality

The University's personality should be:

* Confident
* Thoughtful
* Objective
* Clear
* Professional
* Inclusive
* Consistent

Marketing language should never appear exaggerated or sensational.

* Educational

# Naming Standards

Official institution name:

```text
Agile AI University
```

Abbreviation:

```text
AAU
```

Use "Agile AI" as two separate words.

Do not use:

* AgileAI
* Agile-AI
* AgileAI University

---

# Logo Governance

The University emblem is the primary visual identifier.

Rules:

* Maintain clear space around the emblem.
* Do not distort or rotate.
* Do not recolor without approval.
* Do not place on visually conflicting backgrounds.
* Preserve aspect ratio at all times.

---

# Color Identity

The official color palette is defined in the Design Tokens.

Brand colors shall be consumed through tokens.

Hardcoded brand colors are prohibited.

---

# Typography

Typography shall follow the official Typography Governance.

Alternative fonts are not permitted without governance approval.

---

# Iconography

Icons shall support content.

Icons shall never replace meaningful text.

Icon styling shall remain consistent across all products.

---

# Imagery

Photography and illustrations should communicate:

* learning
* leadership
* collaboration
* technology
* professionalism

Images should avoid cliché corporate stock photography where possible.

---

# Certificates

Certificates represent official institutional recognition.

Every certificate shall display:

* University Emblem
* Official Credential Title
* Learner Name
* Credential Identifier
* Verification Information

Certificate branding shall remain consistent across all credential types.

---

# Digital Badges

Digital badges shall:

* follow approved badge templates
* use official colors
* preserve emblem proportions
* remain recognizable at small sizes

---

# Recognition Assets

# Brand Evolution

Brand evolution shall be intentional and governed.

Changes to visual identity should be:

1. Proposed
2. Reviewed
3. Approved
4. Documented
5. Implemented consistently

---

# Governance Rules

1. Every product shall present a unified institutional identity.

2. Branding shall reinforce Agile AI University's positioning as an independent Academic & Professional Body.

3. Official terminology shall be used consistently.

4. Components shall consume approved Design Tokens.

5. Logos shall not be modified without governance approval.

6. Marketing language shall remain professional and evidence-based.

7. Certificates and recognition assets shall preserve institutional branding.

8. Documentation shall follow the official documentation standard.

9. New products shall comply with this Branding specification before production release.

10. Brand changes require formal governance approval.
