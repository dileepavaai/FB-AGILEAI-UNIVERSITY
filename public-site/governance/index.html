Governance & Institutional Integrity

Agile AI University operates within explicitly defined governance boundaries designed to preserve institutional neutrality, structural integrity, and long-term professional credibility.

Governance is not an auxiliary function of the institution — it is foundational to its architectural design.

Institutional Mandate

Agile AI University functions as an independent institutional authority defining structured capability architecture for AI-enabled agility.

Its mandate is architectural and academic rather than commercial. The institution defines standards, evaluation models, and recognition pathways — it does not operate as a training vendor, certification marketplace, or recruitment intermediary.

Structural Separation of Functions

The institutional architecture maintains separation between:

  • Capability framework definition
  • Assessment instrumentation
  • Executive interpretation layers
  • Professional recognition models
  • Program delivery environments

This separation prevents conflicts of influence and preserves academic independence across institutional functions.

Assessment Governance

Assessment systems are structured multi-dimensional instruments designed for professional insight and orientation.

They do not function as examinations, hiring filters, or automatic certification mechanisms.

Assessment logic, scoring models, and interpretation layers are governed under formal version control protocols.

Credential Governance

Professional recognition pathways operate independently from open assessment mechanisms.

Credentials are issued only under formally defined eligibility, equivalency, and review standards.

Governance provisions include:

  • Defined qualification criteria
  • Ethical compliance requirements
  • Revocation and review procedures
  • Protection against credential dilution

Change Management & Version Control

All institutional surfaces operate under declared structural, content, and assessment model version control.

Structural changes require Structure Version increments. Language revisions require Content Version increments. Assessment logic changes require Assessment Model Version increments.

This framework prevents undocumented architectural drift.

Independence & Commercial Boundaries

Agile AI University maintains clear boundaries between institutional governance and any external commercial learning or implementation environments.

Frameworks and assessments are not positioned as sales instruments or promotional mechanisms.

Institutional Transparency

The institution maintains a formal version registry documenting structural and content evolution across its public surfaces.
