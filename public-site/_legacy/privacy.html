Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the public-facing website of Agile AI University. It does not cover authenticated student portals, administrative systems, or credential verification platforms, which operate under separate access controls.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information automatically through this public website.

Personal information may be voluntarily provided when you contact us via official institutional email addresses for purposes such as:

Any information shared with Agile AI University is used solely for legitimate educational, administrative, or verification purposes.

Communications are handled through secured institutional email systems. Access is restricted to authorized personnel only.

Our website may contain links to external platforms. Agile AI University is not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party websites.
