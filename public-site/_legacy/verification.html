Credential Verification

Agile AI University issues digital credentials to individuals who successfully complete designated programs or assessments.

Credential verification is provided to allow employers, institutions, and third parties to confirm the authenticity of issued credentials.

What Verification Confirms

Verification confirms that:

  • The credential was issued by Agile AI University
  • The credential holder successfully completed the stated program or assessment
  • The credential has not been revoked at the time of verification

What Verification Does Not Confirm

Verification does not:

  • Guarantee employment, promotion, or professional outcomes
  • Indicate regulatory, statutory, or government accreditation unless explicitly stated
  • Imply equivalency to academic degrees or licenses
  • Endorse how a credential is represented or used by the holder

Use of Credentials

Credential holders are responsible for representing their credentials accurately and in context.

Misrepresentation, misuse, or alteration of credentials may result in revocation or public invalidation.

Verification Access

Official verification is performed through Agile AI University’s designated verification platforms or links associated with issued credentials.

Verification results reflect the credential status at the time of inquiry.

Institutional Integrity

Agile AI University maintains credential records to uphold institutional integrity, trust, and transparency.
