# 3. Capability Descriptions

## AAI-CAP-001 — Strategic Alignment

The ability to align AI initiatives with organizational strategy and decision priorities.

---

## AAI-CAP-002 — Intelligent Decision Systems

The infrastructure and processes used to generate data-driven insights through machine intelligence.

---

## AAI-CAP-003 — Adaptive Execution

The ability of operational teams to adapt actions based on new insights and feedback cycles.

---

## AAI-CAP-004 — Human–AI Collaboration

Structured interaction between intelligent systems and human decision-makers.

---

## AAI-CAP-005 — Governance and Responsibility

Organizational mechanisms that ensure responsible and transparent use of AI systems.
