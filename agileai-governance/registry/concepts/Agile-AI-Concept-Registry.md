## Description of Core Concepts

### AAI-C-001 — Agile AI

Agile AI refers to the integration of adaptive execution, machine intelligence, and accountable human judgment to design and operate intelligent organizational systems.

---

### AAI-C-002 — Adaptive Execution

Adaptive execution represents the ability of an organization to continuously adjust plans, workflows, and decisions in response to emerging insights.

---

### AAI-C-003 — Machine Intelligence

Machine intelligence refers to computational systems capable of analyzing data, generating insights, and supporting decision-making through algorithmic models.

---

### AAI-C-004 — Accountable Human Judgment

Accountable human judgment refers to the role of human decision-makers in interpreting AI-generated insights and maintaining responsibility for final decisions.
