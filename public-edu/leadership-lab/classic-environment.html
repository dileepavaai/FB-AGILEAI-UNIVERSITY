Traditional Operational Characteristics

Execution Focus

Operational coordination primarily follows Sprint execution plans and predefined workflows.

Delivery Tracking

Progress visibility is driven through Sprint boards, standups, delivery metrics, and release tracking.

Escalation Handling

Operational disruptions are typically identified after visible execution impact occurs.

Decision Coordination

Operational decision-making is generally role-based and coordination-centered.
