Training Programs

Agile AI University training programs are delivered as instructor-led educational services, conducted online and/or onsite, supported by prerequisite learning materials and guided exercises, strictly in accordance with the published course syllabus.

Enrollment and Commencement

Enrollment is confirmed upon receipt of the first payment or installment. Course delivery is deemed to have commenced once learning materials are shared or a live training session is conducted, whichever occurs first.

The mode of delivery (online or onsite) does not affect the applicability of this refund policy.

Learning Materials

All prerequisite and course-related learning materials are provided after payment confirmation. Once shared or accessed, such materials are considered delivered and are non-returnable and non-reversible.

Training Session Delivery

Attendance in one or more live training sessions constitutes partial delivery of the training program, irrespective of the total number of sessions completed.

Refund Eligibility

Refunds are not applicable once course delivery has commenced. This includes, without limitation, cases where:

  • Learning materials have been shared or accessed, and/or
  • Any live training session has been attended

Partial participation, partial completion, or non-completion of the course does not entitle a participant to a full or proportional refund.

Pauses, Rescheduling, or Interruptions

Temporary pauses or rescheduling due to holidays, instructor availability, health-related reasons, or operational constraints do not negate or reverse training already delivered.

Scope of Training and Excluded Services

Training programs are limited to instructional content and example implementations within the defined syllabus. Requests for customized business advisory, end-to-end solution design, production deployment, or extended real-time debugging constitute separate professional services and are not included in training delivery.

Voluntary Withdrawal

If a participant voluntarily withdraws from a training program after delivery has commenced, all fees paid up to that point remain non-refundable.
