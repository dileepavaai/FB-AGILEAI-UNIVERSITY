# Purpose

This governance standard defines the official institutional language used throughout Agile AI University to communicate the availability of digital services, platforms, capabilities, and learner experiences.

The objective is to preserve a professional, academic, and enterprise-grade institutional identity while avoiding language commonly associated with unfinished or low-maturity software products.

---

# Scope

This standard applies to every Agile AI University digital experience, including but not limited to:

* Student & Executive Portal
* Administration Portal
* Credential Operations Suite
* Assessment Platform
* Learning Journey
* Executive Insights
* Recognition Assets
* Credential Services
* Future Digital Services

---

# Governance Principles

## Principle 1

Agile AI University shall present itself as a mature academic and professional institution.

User interfaces must reinforce institutional confidence rather than product immaturity.

---

## Principle 2

Capabilities shall be described as institutional services rather than software features whenever appropriate.

Preferred terminology includes:

* Service
* Capability
* Platform
* Experience
* Academic Service
* Professional Service

Avoid:

* Feature
* Widget
* Module (user-facing)
* Experimental

---

## Principle 3

Unavailable services shall never be presented as incomplete software.

The institution shall communicate service availability rather than development status.

---

# Approved Service Availability States

## Available

Meaning:

The service is fully operational and accessible to the current user.

Example:

Status

Available

---

## Available in a Future Release

Meaning:

The capability is part of the institutional roadmap but is not yet operational.

This is the preferred wording for planned services.

Example:

Status

Available in a Future Release

---

## Invitation Required

Meaning:

The service exists but requires invitation or institutional approval.

Example:

Executive Advisory Services

Status

Invitation Required

---

## Restricted Access

Meaning:

The service exists but requires an entitlement, credential, subscription, or membership level not currently held by the user.

Example:

Executive Insights

Status

Restricted Access

---

## Temporarily Unavailable

Meaning:

The service normally exists but is temporarily unavailable because of maintenance or operational issues.

Example:

Assessment Platform

Status

Temporarily Unavailable

---

# Prohibited User-Facing Language

The following expressions shall not appear in production user interfaces.

* Coming Soon
* Under Construction
* Beta
* Alpha
* Experimental
* Preview
* Prototype
* Work in Progress
* In Development

These terms reduce institutional credibility and are inconsistent with the Agile AI University brand.

---

# Presentation Standard

Where a service is unavailable, the interface shall:

* explain the purpose of the service;
* communicate its availability state using an approved status;
* avoid presenting disabled functionality without context;
* avoid creating the perception of unfinished software.

Example:

Executive Insights

AI-driven executive reports derived from your credentials, assessments, and lifelong learning achievements.

Status

Available in a Future Release

---

# Institutional Positioning

This governance standard reinforces the positioning of Agile AI University as:

* an independent academic institution;
* a professional credentialing authority;
* an enterprise-grade digital ecosystem;
* a governance-driven platform.

The user experience shall always reflect institutional maturity rather than software development progress.

---

# Governance Decision

The Service Availability Governance Standard is hereby designated as a LOCKED governance decision.

All future portals, services, applications, and digital experiences shall conform to this standard unless superseded by a later approved governance revision.
