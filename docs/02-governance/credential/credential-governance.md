# Purpose

This document defines the governance framework for all credentials issued by Agile AI University.

It establishes the principles, lifecycle, ownership, publication rules, verification standards, programme evolution policies, and historical preservation strategy governing every credential issued by the university.

This is the authoritative governance document for the entire Agile AI ecosystem.

# Governance Principles

The Agile AI University credential ecosystem follows five principles.

## 1. Credentials are Permanent

A credential represents a learner's achievement at a specific point in time.

Once issued it must never be altered.

Historical achievements are preserved forever.

---

## 2. Programmes Evolve

Programmes may

- evolve
- improve
- split
- merge
- retire

without affecting previously issued credentials.

Programme lifecycle and credential lifecycle are independent.

---

## 3. Recognition Evolves

Professional recognition may evolve throughout a learner's career.

Recognition does not invalidate previous credentials.

Recognition extends the learner's professional journey.

---

## 4. Registry is the Source of Truth

Every credential must exist in the Credential Registry.

No credential may exist outside the registry.

The registry governs

- identity
- ownership
- verification
- publication
- lifecycle

---

## 5. Verification is Permanent

Every credential remains verifiable regardless of

- programme retirement
- curriculum changes
- branding changes
- future programme versions

Verification links never expire.

# Programme Lifecycle

Programme lifecycle is independent.

```
Programme Created

↓

Programme Active

↓

Programme Improved

↓

Programme Superseded

↓

Programme Retired

↓

Historical Reference
```

Programme retirement does not invalidate historical credentials.

---

# Historical Credential Policy

Historical credentials remain

- authentic
- valid
- verifiable
- downloadable
- shareable

for life.

Historical credentials are never rewritten to newer programme names.

---

# Founding Credential Policy

Founding programmes are preserved permanently.

They represent the evolution of Agile AI University.

Examples include

- Agile Outcome Practitioner (AOP)

Founding credentials become part of the university's historical archive.

---

# Retired Programme Policy

Retired programmes

- stop accepting registrations
- stop issuing new credentials
- remain visible historically
- remain verifiable
- remain recognised

Retirement never deletes history.

---

# Programme Successor Policy

A programme may define

Current Programme

↓

Successor Programme

Example

```
AOP

↓

AIPA

↓

AAIP

↓

AIAL
```

Successor programmes are advisory.

They do not replace historical credentials.

---

# Professional Recognition

Learners may obtain additional recognitions throughout their career.

Recognition supplements credentials.

Recognition never overwrites credentials.

Examples

- Distinguished Professional
- Executive Fellow
- Certified Trainer
- Industry Recognition

---

# Credential Identity

Each credential has a permanent identity.

Example

```
AAU-KO9JK02Z
```

Credential IDs are immutable.

---

# Credential Registry

The registry stores

- Credential ID
- Learner
- Programme
- Credential Type
- Status
- Verification Metadata
- Published Assets
- Recognition Relationships
- Lifecycle Information

The registry is the authoritative source.

---

# Asset Governance

Generated assets include

- University Certificate
- Digital Badge
- Trainer Certificate
- Recognition Certificate

Assets are generated by the Admin Portal.

Student Portal never generates assets.

Student Portal only consumes published assets.

---

# Asset Publication Governance

Asset publication follows

```
Generate

↓

Upload Storage

↓

Publish Registry

↓

Student Consumption
```

Only published assets are visible to learners.

---

# Student Portal Responsibilities

Student Portal

✓ Authenticate

✓ Authorize

✓ Resolve Entitlements

✓ Retrieve Credential

✓ Retrieve Published Assets

✓ Preview

✓ Download

✓ Share

Student Portal must never generate credentials.

---

# Admin Portal Responsibilities

Admin Portal

✓ Generate Assets

✓ Publish Assets

✓ Upload Storage

✓ Update Registry

✓ Maintain Templates

✓ Audit Publication

---

# Verification Governance

Verification must always display

- Credential ID
- Learner Name
- Programme
- Status
- Issuing Authority

Verification remains available permanently.

---

# Immutability Rules

The following fields must never change after issuance.

- Credential ID
- Learner Identity
- Issue Record
- Issuing Authority
- Historical Programme
- Original Credential Type

---

# Evolution Rules

The following may evolve.

- Recognition
- Career Status
- Executive Recognition
- Alumni Status
- Wallet Support
- Sharing Features
- Published Assets

---

# Governance Decisions

The following decisions are permanently locked.

## Decision 001

Credentials are permanent.

## Decision 002

Programmes may retire.

## Decision 003

Programme retirement never invalidates credentials.

## Decision 004

Historical credentials are never renamed.

## Decision 005

Recognition supplements credentials.

## Decision 006

Credential Registry is the single source of truth.

## Decision 007

Student Portal never generates credential assets.

## Decision 008

Admin Portal exclusively generates and publishes credential assets.

## Decision 009

Verification remains available for life.

## Decision 010

Founding credentials are preserved as part of Agile AI University's institutional history.
