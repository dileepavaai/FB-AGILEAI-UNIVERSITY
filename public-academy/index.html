Credential Registry and Governance

All credentials within the Agile AI ecosystem are defined and governed under the Agile AI Credential Registry.

Each credential is assigned a permanent identifier (AAI-CR-XXX), ensuring traceability, verification integrity, and long-term continuity.

Credential identifiers remain preserved even when credentials are retired, maintaining historical continuity.
