# Agile AI Program Registry

!!! success "Specification Status — CANONICAL"
This document is part of the official **Agile AI Specification** and represents the canonical registry of professional capability pathways within the Agile AI ecosystem.

**DOCUMENT TYPE:** Registry  
**VERSION:** 1.0  
**STATUS:** CANONICAL  
**MAINTAINED BY:** Agile AI University  

---

# 1. Purpose

This registry defines the official **professional capability pathways** within the Agile AI ecosystem.

Programs represent structured pathways through which experienced professionals strengthen their capability to operate within Agile AI systems.

Programs are not introductory learning courses or training programs.

Instead, they represent **professional capability pathways aligned with the Agile AI ecosystem architecture**.

---

# 2. Program Tracks

Programs operate across three primary tracks.

| Track                | Focus                                                                 |
|---------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **P — Professional** | Development of core Agile AI professional capability                 |
| **M — Master**       | Advanced capability in designing and governing Agentic AI systems    |
| **L — Leadership**   | Strategic leadership in AI-enabled organizational environments       |

# 4. Program Structure

Programs provide structured environments for professionals to strengthen their capability in applying Agile AI principles within real organizational contexts.

Programs may include:

- applied capability development  
- real-world organizational scenarios  
- peer collaboration and dialogue  
- capability assessments  
- credential recognition pathways  

Programs are designed primarily for **experienced professionals operating in complex organizational environments**.

---

# 5. Relationship to Credentials

Programs provide structured pathways that may lead to credential recognition.

### Professional Track Progression

Agile AI Professional Capability Pathway  
↓  
Artificial Intelligence Professional Agilist (AIPA)

### Master Track Progression

Agentic AI Systems Capability Pathway  
↓  
Agentic Artificial Intelligence Agilist (AAIA)  
↓  
Agentic Artificial Intelligence Master (AAIM)  
↓  
Agile Artificial Intelligence Capability Coach (AAICC)

### Leadership Track Progression

Agile AI Leadership Capability Pathway  
↓  
AI Strategic Leadership (AISL)  
AI Organizational Leadership (AIOL)  
AI Product Leadership (AIPL)

Programs therefore represent the **capability development layer of the ecosystem**.

# 6. Program Identifier Format

Program identifiers follow a structured format.

**AAI-PR-XXX**

| Component | Meaning                       |
|-----------|-------------------------------|
| AAI       | Agile AI ecosystem identifier |
| PR        | Program Registry              |
| XXX       | Sequential program number     |

## Example

| Identifier | Meaning                                     |
|------------|---------------------------------------------|
| AAI-PR-001 | First program defined in the registry       |
| AAI-PR-002 | Second program defined in the registry      |

Program identifiers remain permanent once assigned.

---

# 7. Governance Notes

- Program IDs are permanent and must never be reused  
- Programs must never be removed from the registry  
- Registry must support traceability and ecosystem referencing  
- Programs must align with capability frameworks and credential pathways  
- Any additions must follow the established identifier format  

---

# 8. Governance Authority

Programs defined in this registry are governed by **Agile AI University**.

Program identifiers remain stable to ensure consistency across:

- institutional documentation  
- capability frameworks  
- credential pathways  
- ecosystem references
