# Credential Consumption Experience

**Version:** 1.0

**Status:** GOVERNANCE LOCKED

**Date:** June 2026

---

# Executive Summary

The Credential Consumption Experience defines how university members discover, understand, consume, share and progress from their academic credentials within the Agile AI University ecosystem.

The experience extends beyond displaying certificates. It provides a professional, learner-centric experience that combines academic achievements with contextual learning opportunities while preserving the integrity of university credentials.

The Credential Registry remains the single source of truth for all credential information.

---

# Vision

Provide every university member with a professional, trustworthy and engaging credential experience that supports lifelong learning and capability development.

The portal shall become the primary destination for consuming university credentials and understanding future academic opportunities.

---

# Objectives

The Credential Consumption Experience shall enable members to

- Discover earned credentials
- Understand academic achievements
- Access recognition assets
- Verify credential authenticity
- Share professional achievements
- Explore future learning opportunities
- Maintain lifelong engagement with the university

# Core Principles

## Principle 1

Credentials are permanent academic achievements.

The portal consumes credentials.

The portal never edits credentials.

---

## Principle 2

Credential information originates from the Credential Registry.

The portal never becomes the source of credential data.

---

## Principle 3

Every credential shall be presented consistently across the Agile AI University ecosystem.

Consistency applies to

- Student Portal
- Executive Portal
- Verification Portal
- Mobile Applications
- Future University Services

---

## Principle 4

Credential presentation shall always be contextual.

A credential may display

- Recognition assets
- Verification information
- Learning opportunities
- Bridge opportunities
- Related programs

Context shall never modify the credential itself.

# Credential Card Governance

Every credential shall be displayed as a reusable component.

Credential Cards shall contain

- Credential Name
- Program Name
- Status
- Credential ID
- Available Assets
- Actions

Credential Cards shall not contain

- Administrative controls
- Editing capabilities
- Operational workflow actions

Credentials may display contextual recommendations.

Bridge Program Available

Recommended Program

# Bridge Program Governance

Bridge opportunities shall appear only when determined by resolver output.

Example

Agile Outcome Practitioner

Bridge Opportunity Available

Upgrade to Artificial Intelligence Professional Agilist

[ Learn More ]

The recommendation is contextual.

The credential remains unchanged.

---

# Credential Portfolio Governance

Members own a Credential Portfolio.

The portfolio represents every university achievement.

Credentials are never hidden because newer credentials exist.

Example

✓ Agile Outcome Practitioner

✓ Artificial Intelligence Professional Agilist

✓ Agentic AI Agilist

Each credential remains independently visible.

# Overall Position

The Credential Consumption Experience establishes a consistent, learner-centric approach to presenting university credentials across the Agile AI University ecosystem.

Credentials remain immutable academic achievements.

The portal enriches those achievements with contextual learning opportunities, recognition assets and future academic pathways while preserving the Credential Registry as the single source of truth.

This governance enables the Student & Executive Portal to evolve from a credential viewer into a lifelong professional portfolio and academic engagement platform without compromising academic integrity.
