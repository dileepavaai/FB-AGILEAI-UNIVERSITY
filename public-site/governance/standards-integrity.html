Governance & Institutional Policies

Agile AI University operates as an independent academic and professional body. All academic frameworks, assessments, credentials, and recognitions are governed under formally defined policies to ensure integrity, independence, and professional credibility.

Institutional Authority

Agile AI University defines, maintains, and governs its own academic frameworks, capability models, assessment systems, and credentialing structures.

Authority is exercised through:

  • Published academic frameworks and reference models
  • Documented assessment and credential criteria
  • Independent academic and professional oversight

Independence & Non-Commercial Posture

Agile AI University is not a training vendor, coaching marketplace, or certification sales platform.

Academic definitions, assessments, and credentials are not influenced by:

  • Training enrollments or course purchases
  • Marketing partnerships or sponsorships
  • Individual instructors or commercial providers

Assessment Governance

Capability assessments provided by Agile AI University are diagnostic and reflective instruments.

Assessments are designed to:

  • Support professional self-reflection
  • Identify capability patterns and development directions
  • Inform potential academic or professional pathways

Assessments do not constitute examinations and do not directly confer credentials.

Credential Governance

Professional credentials issued by Agile AI University are governed recognitions of advanced capability.

Credential issuance is subject to:

  • Defined academic or professional criteria
  • Program-specific evaluation standards
  • Formal issuance and verification controls

Credentials are issued only under approved Professional (P), Master (M), or Leadership (L) tracks.

Reference IDs & Traceability

Reports, credentials, and formal recognitions may carry a reference ID to support traceability and audit clarity.

Reference IDs indicate document lineage and issuance context. They do not imply certification status unless explicitly stated.

Data Use & Privacy

Participant data collected through assessments or academic interactions is used strictly for academic, professional, or institutional purposes.

Agile AI University does not sell, trade, or commercially exploit participant data.

Framework Evolution

Academic frameworks, assessment models, and credential structures may evolve over time to reflect advances in:

  • Agile practice
  • Artificial Intelligence systems
  • Agentic AI architectures
  • Organizational governance models

Evolution is governed institutionally and documented transparently.
